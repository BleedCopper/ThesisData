A Model for Age and Gender Profiling
of Social Media Accounts Based on Post Contents
What is it about?

It is a study that aims to reduce anonymity by identifying the age and gender of social media accounts.
By reducing online anonymity, we aim to reduce cybercrimes and support business intelligence.

Help in need...

However, to build the system, we need thousands of tweets from Twitter.

With this, may we ask for your aid in collecting this data?
Rest assured that we respect your privacy, and will remove your identity
from the posts and tweets that we will be able to gather from your accounts.
