WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

It is a study that aims to reduce anonymity by
identifying the age and gender of social media accounts.

By reducing online anonymity,
we aim to reduce cybercrimes and support business intelligence.

WILL YOU HELP US?

However, to build the system, we need thousands of tweets from Twitter.

With this, may we ask for your aid in collecting this data?
Rest assured that we respect your privacy, and will remove your identity
from the posts and tweets that will be gathered from your accounts.
